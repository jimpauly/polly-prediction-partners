# Privacy — Paulie's Prediction Partners

**Contact:** chickensaurusrex@outlook.com

---

## The short version

**We collect nothing. Your data stays on your computer.**

- No analytics or usage tracking
- No telemetry sent anywhere
- No ads
- No user accounts on our end
- No cookies
- The only outside network traffic happens when _you_ choose to connect to Kalshi's API

---

## What stays on your machine

The following is stored locally and never sent to us:

| Data                          | Where                                       |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| Theme and display preferences | Local browser/Electron storage              |
| Illumination control settings | Local browser/Electron storage              |
| Setup wizard completion state | `userData/wizard-state.json` on your device |
| To-Do list items              | Local browser storage                       |

You can wipe all of this by clearing your app data or uninstalling.

## Your Kalshi credentials

Your Kalshi API key and RSA private key:

- Live in memory only while the app is running — never written to disk
- Are never sent to us or anyone else
- Are cleared from memory when you disconnect or close the app
- Show as `****` in the UI with only the last 4 characters visible

When you connect to Kalshi, your credentials go **directly from your computer to Kalshi's servers** (api.kalshi.com). We're not in the middle of that.
