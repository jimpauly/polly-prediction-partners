# Paulie's Prediction Partners — User Guide

This app places trades in prediction markets on Kalshi using automated agents.
You provide your Kalshi credentials, choose a trading mode, and the agents do the rest.

---

## Before You Start

You need a Kalshi account. Sign up at **kalshi.com** if you don't have one.

Once logged in to Kalshi, go to your account settings and generate an API key.
You will need two things from there: your **API Key** and your **Private Key**.
Copy them somewhere you can find them.

---

## Getting the App

**Use the website** (nothing to install):
Go to **jimpauly.github.io/paulies-prediction-partners** in any web browser.

**Download the desktop app** (Windows or Mac):
Go to **github.com/jimpauly/paulies-prediction-partners/releases**.
Download the file for your computer. Run it and follow the prompts.

---

## First-Time Setup

When the app opens for the first time, it will walk you through a short setup.
Paste in your Kalshi API Key and Private Key when asked.
The app does not share your keys with anyone.

---

## Trading Modes

Set your mode in the **Agent Dashboard** before the agents do anything.

| Mode      | What happens                                 |
| --------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| Auto      | Agents trade on their own without asking you |
| Semi-Auto | Agents ask you to approve each trade first   |
| Safe      | No trades are placed                         |

Start in **Safe** mode to look around before anything is live.

---

## Basic Use

1. Open the **Trading Studio** to see available markets.
2. Go to the **Agent Dashboard** to turn agents on or off and set the mode.
3. The right panel shows your open positions and trade history.
4. The **Global Throttle** in the bottom bar controls how actively the agents trade.
   Slide it down to reduce activity.
